Professional Summary

Computational biologist and bioinformatician with 7+ years of experience translating bulk, single-cell, and spatial transcriptomics into decision-grade insights for oncology. Builds end-to-end, reproducible R/Python pipelines (Seurat/Scanpy), multi-cohort integration, differential/perturbation analyses, and HPC-scaled workflows. Technical depth includes tumor–microenvironment dissection, CNV-based malignancy inference, cell-state annotation, ligand–receptor signaling inference, and spatial integration with histology.
